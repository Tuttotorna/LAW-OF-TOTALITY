# World Introduction Strategy — v0.6.0

## Purpose

This document defines how the Law of Totality should be introduced externally without weakening the core and without triggering immediate rejection through excessive initial claims.

## Strategic Principle

Do not begin with:

    this explains everything
    this corrects humanity
    this is the absolute law of error

Begin with:

    local correctness does not guarantee valid use

Then show the formula.

Then show cases.

Let the scope become visible through repeated application.

## Internal Core

The internal core remains:

    every finite treatment of x produces a local framework F_A(x)
    every F_A(x) has a valid scope
    structural error begins when F_A(x) is used beyond that scope while excluding dependencies critical to the actual use

## External Door

The external door is:

> A compact formula for detecting when a locally correct model, answer, decision, or theory becomes structurally invalid because it is used beyond its valid scope.

## First Audiences

1. AI safety and AI governance
2. model risk and risk management
3. systems safety and STPA/STAMP communities
4. decision theory and institutional audit
5. philosophy of science and epistemology
6. broader public communication only after technical traction

## What To Show First

Do not show the total philosophical architecture first.

Show:

    formula
    one AI case
    one model-risk case
    one medical/legal case
    one negative control
    the Error Atlas

## Correct Public Claim

> The Law of Totality does not claim to explain every thing as content. It explains the structural validity of finite treatments: object, framework, use, valid scope, and excluded critical dependencies.

## Incorrect Public Claim

Avoid:

    proves the cause of all error
    claims total resolution without validation
    replaces all existing disciplines
    no one has ever understood error before
    all existing work is wrong

These create unnecessary rejection and are not needed.

## Strategic Goal

Make the world say:

    this is useful
    this detects a real failure pattern
    this applies across domains
    this compresses something we currently treat separately

Once that happens, the deeper scope becomes visible without overstatement.
