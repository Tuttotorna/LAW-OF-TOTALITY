## What Not To Ask

Do not ask reviewers to confirm importance.

Ask reviewers to find failure.

## Desired Output

Each reviewer should return:

    strongest existing equivalent;
    strongest objection;
    weakest definition;
    best use case;
    worst use case;
    required next step.
