# External Review Packet — v0.7.0

## Purpose

This packet defines how the repository should be submitted to reviewers.

## Recommended Reviewers

1. AI risk / AI governance reviewer
2. Model risk management reviewer
3. Systems safety / STPA reviewer
4. Philosophy of science or epistemology reviewer
5. Formal methods or logic reviewer

## What To Ask

Do not ask:

    Is this revolutionary?
    Is this a universal law?
    Did I discover the error of humanity?

Ask:

    Is the formula coherent?
    Is the object-framework-use distinction useful?
    Does the Error Atlas map cases correctly?
    Is the comparison with existing frameworks fair?
    What is already known?
    What is genuinely added?
    What would make this externally valid?
    Where does the formula fail?
    Which cases are forced or weak?
    What definitions must be hardened?

## Review Form

### 1. Coherence

Does the framework maintain internal consistency?

### 2. Novelty

### 3. Operational Usefulness

Could this help audit AI answers, models, decisions, policies, or institutional reasoning?

    yes / no / partly

Comments:

### 4. Failure Cases

Give at least three examples where the formula is weak, ambiguous, or unnecessary.

### 5. Strongest Neighboring Framework

Which existing framework is closest?

    NIST AI RMF
    model risk management
    STPA/STAMP
    systems thinking
    map-territory
    other

### 6. Required Hardening

What must be improved before this can be cited, published, or used?

## Review Request Text

Subject:

    Request for hostile technical review: Structural Error Formula / Law of Totality

Body:

    I am requesting a hostile technical review of a compact structural-error framework.

    The framework does not claim external validation as a universal law.

    Its central claim is narrower:

        local correctness does not guarantee valid use;
        structural error appears when a framework F is used as sufficient for an actual use U while excluding a dependency critical to U.

    I am specifically asking whether this is:
        already known under another framework;
        a useful compression of existing ideas;
        formally weak;
        operationally useful;
        overclaimed;
        worth developing further.

    The most relevant files are:
        FORMAL_SYSTEM.md
        ERROR_FORMULA.md
        OPERATIONAL_AUDIT.md
        ERROR_ATLAS.md
        COMPARATIVE_ANALYSIS.md
        DIFFERENCE_MATRIX.md

    I am looking for criticism, not endorsement.

## Correct Attitude

External review is not validation hunting.

It is attack surface discovery.
