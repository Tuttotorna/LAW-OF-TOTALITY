# Human Dilemmas Example — Hardened Version

## Formula

~~~text

LocalClosureF(x) ∧ ScopeViolationΩ(F,x)
~~~

A human dilemma often arises when a finite mind demands total closure from a field that exists as dependence.

---

## Examples

~~~text
freedom / security
individual / collective
justice / mercy
truth / usefulness
life / death
war / peace
technology / humanity
nature / civilization
~~~

These are not closed binaries.

They are relational fields.

---

## Hardened Sentence

The dilemma does not always persist because no answer exists.

Often it persists because the answer is being searched inside a false closure.

But false closure is a diagnostic hypothesis, not an automatic explanation of every unresolved problem.
